Advanced Level

The Advanced Level course is tailored for those who are already proficient in music theory and wish to refine their skills further. This course delves into intricate topics such as:

  • Complex harmony and advanced chord progressions:
    Analyze sophisticated harmonic structures, including extended chords and modal interchange.
  • Orchestration and instrumentation:
    Explore the techniques of arranging music for different instruments and ensembles.
  • Music analysis and composition techniques:
    Study the works of renowned composers to understand their methods and apply these techniques in your compositions.
  • Advanced counterpoint:
    Examine the principles of counterpoint at a higher level, composing intricate polyphonic textures.
  • Music theory in contemporary genres:
    Analyze modern styles such as jazz, pop, and electronic music, applying theoretical concepts to these genres.
  • Capstone project:
    Complete a final project that showcases your understanding and application of advanced music theory concepts.

This course provides an in-depth understanding of advanced music theory, paving the way for further musical exploration and professional development.
