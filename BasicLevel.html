Basic Level

The Basic Level course is designed for beginners who want to learn the fundamentals of music theory.
It covers essential topics such as:

  • Understanding musical notation:
    Learn how to read and write music, including notes, rests, and measures.
  • Learning about rhythm and meter:
    Discover how to identify different time signatures and rhythmic patterns.
  • Identifying notes and scales:
    Understand the different musical scales and how to identify notes on the staff.
  • Exploring simple melodies and harmonies:
    Gain skills in creating basic melodies and harmonies using the knowledge acquired.

This course provides a solid foundation for further studies in music theory and practice. Join us to start your musical journey today!
